How Your Gift Helps

In 2016, Team Member Giving made possible...

  • 14 vein finders that provide improved speed and quality of patient care

  • Post-partum equipment to effectively handle pregnancy-related complications

  • Four neonatal and pediatric intubation machines to better serve approx. 85,000 infants, children and adolescents annually

  • Aid to 30 team members who experienced catastrophic events

  • 45 educational scholarships to support team members pursuing high education

  • Innovative operating room technology that increases success rates, reduces insertion attempts and decreases complications

  • Needed comprehensive care support

  • Community hospice support to assist in providing compassionate medical, psychological and spiritual support to patients and their families

  • 80 infant car seats, 500 bike helmets, 250 floatation devices to kids in WellStar communities

  • 911 mammograms and services for underserved and uninsured patients
